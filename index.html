Thai Chess, known as Makruk, is an ancient variant of chess that originated in Thailand. It's considered one of the closest relatives to the original Indian Chaturanga. The game features unique pieces like the Met (similar to a queen but with limited movement) and Sin (a bishop that moves only one square diagonally). Makruk is known for its strategic depth and faster-paced gameplay compared to international chess, making it both challenging and exciting for players of all levels.
